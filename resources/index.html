Our Background

We started our journey, our first adventure in Tuscany region in Italy. As a big family we started producing finest wines from our finest vineyards. Tuscany region in Italy is indeed famous because of it's high concentration of vineyards, wineries and wine houses. As our wine business grew stronger, we started opening more and more wineries all over the Italy. First foreign winery was opened in France, Epernay. We started growing accross France, expanded our winery chain to Spain, England, Ireland and Switzerland. With this amount of wineries accross Europe, production of our finest wines tripled.

As soon as we reached our demanded goal of total amout wineries accross Europe, wine exportation started all over the world. First aim was to export wine in USA. As we did. From New York to Boston and Los Angeles. Aside from USA, people demanded our wine to be exported accross Asia and Australia. From Tokyo and Moscow to Melbourne and Sydney. Later date, we introduced feedback system to our customers. Customers are able to provide feedback or even suggest ideas on new wines. We created many custom demanded wines for our customers, leaving them happy and satisfied.
